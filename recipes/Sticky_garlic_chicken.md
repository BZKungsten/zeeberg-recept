recipediarywithalora Sticky Garlic Chicken & Veggie Stir-Fry
Ingredients:
 500g chicken breast or thigh, bite-sized pieces
 1 green bell pepper, sliced
 1 red bell pepper, sliced
 1 small onion, sliced
 2–3 green onions, chopped
 3 cloves garlic, minced
 1 tsp ginger, minced
 2 tbsp oil
 1 tbsp sesame seeds
Sauce:
 1/4 cup soy sauce
 2 tbsp honey or brown sugar
 1 tbsp oyster sauce
 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 3 tbsp water
 1 tsp sesame oil
 1/2 tsp black pepper
 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
Directions:
Heat oil in a pan or wok and cook chicken until golden and fully cooked. Add garlic and ginger and sauté until fragrant. Toss in onions and bell peppers and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes so they stay slightly crisp. In a bowl, mix all sauce ingredients, then pour into the pan. Stir well and cook until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken beautifully. Finish with green onions and sesame seeds. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
